How To Buy

Step 1 : Category and Cart

First you have to go to Products page. And then choose the product Category by clicking on the catergory bar similar to the picture above

And then just click the Add to Cart button of the product that you want to add it to your Cart

Step 2 : Filling Forms and Order products

Before you click the Order Now button make sure you have fill up the required form like Name, Address, and Phone Number correctly. Whenever you're done selecting the product and then you just need to click the order now button

And there would be a Pop up showing a message to click Send in the gmail page and wait for the Wengridient owner confirmation, just click Ok and you will be redirected to Gmail.

Step 3 : Sending order email, Owner confirmation, and Payment

After you clicking the Order Now button you will redirected to gmail and the text body and receiver will be filled automaticaly, You can add some notes to the message or edit your data before clicking SEND

After you click the send button please wait for Confirmation calls or email by the Wengridient owner about the delivery and the total price if there is a discount when you order

And the payment will be also confirmed after the owner confirm your order by calls or email, usually the payment is using Transfer or Cost on Delivery,COD methos is only available after you have a valid personal data gived to the Wengridient

You can check about the Delivery info at Wengridient Home page
